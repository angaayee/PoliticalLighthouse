About the firm

Transforming political vision into electoral success.

Political Lighthouse is a political consultancy built by former field directors, pollsters, and digital strategists who were tired of watching good candidates lose with bad messaging. We help political leaders, parties, elected representatives, and aspiring candidates achieve measurable success.

From campaign planning to voter outreach, digital strategy, constituency management, and public engagement, we've managed statewide election campaigns, coordinated with Ministers, MLAs, MPs, and District Secretaries, and run the social media and digital operations behind races that came from behind to win.
